# Goals Roadmap: Horizontal Scroll Missing/Sticky

Problem
- Goals Roadmap view does not support expected horizontal scrolling; users cannot pan timeline fluidly.

Desired
- Ensure container has overflow-x auto with visible scrollbar styling.
- Support shift+wheel horizontal scroll and click-and-drag panning (like Roadmap V3 elsewhere).
- Keep header sticky and bars interactive; preserve drag/resize UX.

Acceptance
- Mouse wheel + Shift pans horizontally.
- Trackpad two-finger pan works.
- Visible scrollbar appears on hover.
- No layout jump or lag while dragging.
